❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Elysia

How do I add Elysia to my server?
You can add Elysiato your server by clicking the "Invite to Server" button at the top of this page or by using the .invite command in any server where Elysiais already present. You'll need "Manage Server" permissions to add the bot.
Why can't Elysia join my voice channel?
There could be several reasons:
  • You may not be in a voice channel
  • Elysiamay not have permission to join your voice channel
  • The bot may be at maximum server capacity (for free version)
  • There could be a temporary Discord issue
Try using the .join command while in a voice channel, and ensure the bot has the necessary permissions.
What are the benefits of Premium?
Premium unlocks several exclusive features:
  • Higher audio quality (up to 320kbps)
  • Priority song queueing
  • Custom playlists and saved tracks
  • Advanced audio filters and effects
  • Lyrics display for current tracks
  • No ads or sponsor messages
  • Priority support
  • Song downloading
  • Custom announcements
Use .premium to check your status or .buy to purchase.
How do I change the bot's prefix?
Server administrators can change the prefix using the .prefix [new prefix] command. For example: .prefix ! will change the prefix to "!". The default prefix is ".".
Why is my music skipping or lagging?
Music skipping or lagging can be caused by:
  • Internet connection issues
  • High server load
  • Too many filters/effects applied
  • Regional restrictions on content
Try these solutions:
  • Check your internet connection
  • Reduce audio quality with .quality medium
  • Remove filters with .reset
  • Join our support server for further help
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
You can report bugs or suggest features by:
  • Using the .report [message] command
  • Joining our support server
  • Contacting the developer directly via DM
Please include as much detail as possible when reporting issues, including steps to reproduce the problem.
How can I support Elysia's development?
There are several ways to support Elysia:

  • Join our support server
  • Share Elysiawith your friends and communities
  • Provide feedback and suggestions
Your support helps keep Elysiarunning and allows us to add more features!
